Available Services

Electronic Tickets
  1. Send your tickets to the Kloud while printing or from ticket data stores.
  2. Tickets can be easily accessed through a website or mobile application.
  3. Drivers, customers, and DOT can access relevant tickets via account login
  4. Automate Ticket emailing at various times of the day to comply with changing DOT requirements.
  5. Drivers and other users can be messaged through the web application and website.
Data Reporting
  1. Monitor and Analyze your business operations based on your data.
  2. Monitor truck tare weights, underfilled and overfilled loads, total job deliveries over time and more.
  3. Work with us to build custom dashboards to suit your business needs.
GPS Tracking
  1. By simply logging in to website or mobile app, our services can pull near real-time GPS information from drivers.
  2. Visualize the real-time locations of current shipments and trucks.
  3. Monitor your trucks through our Reporting Service. Optimize plant shut down times to prevent driver waiting, alert customers when a delivery is near, monitor and record delivery times, and more.
Incident Capture
  1. Mount cameras to your trucks or in facilities.
  2. Send the video feed to the Kloud for storage in case of an incident.
