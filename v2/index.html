Services

Our software solution can be painlessly and seamlessly integrated into your current workflow and devices, making your data readily accessible and sharable through a website or mobile application. Open a new avenue of possibilities with our services; contact us today to build a custom solution for you.

Electronic Tickets

Send your tickets to the cloud while printing or from ticket data stores. Tickets can be easily accessed through a website or mobile application.

Data Reporting

Monitor and Analyze your business operations based on your data. Monitor truck tare weights, underfilled and overfilled loads, total job deliveries over time and more. Work with us to build custom dashboards to suit your business needs.

GPS Tracking

From a website or mobile app, visualize the real-time locations of current shipments and trucks. Optimize plant shut down times, prevent driver waiting, alert customers when a delivery is near, monitor and record delivery times, and more.

Incident Capture

Mount cameras to your trucks or in facilities. Send the video feed to the cloud for storage in case of an incident.

DOT Reporting

DOT Tracking and Reporting including support for DOT required VETA uploads.
